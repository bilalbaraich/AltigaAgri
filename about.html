Our Story

New Roots, Strong Vision for Pakistan's Agriculture

Altiga Agri Solutions (Pvt.) Ltd. is a dynamic and forward-thinking company committed to advancing Pakistan's agriculture. Backed by industry expertise and a passion for progress, we deliver high-quality solutions that meet the evolving needs of modern farmers.

Our state-of-the-art formulation and packaging plant specializes in the precise production of SC, EC, and granular pesticides—ensuring high quality and consistency in every product. We place utmost importance on honesty and integrity, building strong trust with Pakistan's farming community.

Advanced Production Capabilities

Our manufacturing facility is equipped with modern technology for producing various pesticide formulations. Every batch undergoes rigorous quality testing to ensure consistency, efficacy, and safety.

  • SC (Suspension Concentrates) – Stable, easy-to-use liquid formulations with superior efficacy
  • EC (Emulsifiable Concentrates) – Oil-based solutions for effective pest control
  • WG (Water Dispersible Granules) – Dust-free, easy-to-measure granule formulations
  • WP (Wettable Powder) – Economical and highly effective formulations
  • SL & SE Formulations – Soluble liquids and suspension emulsions for precision delivery
